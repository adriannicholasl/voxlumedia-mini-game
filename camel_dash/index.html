🐪 Camel Dash — Desert Run

A fast, top-down arcade runner. Steer your camel through the Egyptian desert, collect dates, and avoid obstacles. Reach the best score in 60 seconds.

How to Play

  • Move: ← / →
  • Dash: ↑ (short speed boost, 3s cooldown)
  • Start/Restart: Enter
  • Pause/Resume: P  |  Hide/Show Panel: H
  • Scoring: +5 per date, passive points over time
